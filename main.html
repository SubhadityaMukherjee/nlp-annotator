• AnnotateNLP!

This is an Open Source annotator for NLP.
Using this is really simple.
Just enter all your data in the following box. Each sentence in one line.
Also mention the tag you want
After that all you need to do is highlight the word with your cursor and click the button.
You will be presented with a formatted json at the end
Happy Tagging!
